Meet the director

Gez

Director of Light Up · Hong Kong

Gez is the Director of Light Up, a Hong Kong–based wellbeing company with divisions across China. He leads the company with a simple belief: true health is more than physical strength—it is harmony between the body, soul and heart.

Guided by compassion and love, Light Up helps people build healthier bodies, find greater inner balance and reconnect with what gives life meaning. Gez’s vision is to create a community where wellbeing feels human, wholehearted and accessible—and where every person is empowered to live with more energy, purpose and love.
